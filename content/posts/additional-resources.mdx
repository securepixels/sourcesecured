### YouTube Channels
- **[Professor Messer](https://www.youtube.com/user/professormesser)**: Offers free training videos for CompTIA certifications and other IT topics.
- **[Cyber Mentor](https://www.youtube.com/c/TheCyberMentor)**: Provides tutorials and walkthroughs on ethical hacking and penetration testing.
- **[NetworkChuck](https://www.youtube.com/c/NetworkChuck)**: Focuses on networking and cybersecurity concepts in an engaging and entertaining way.
- **[HackerSploit](https://www.youtube.com/c/HackerSploit)**: Offers ethical hacking tutorials and penetration testing techniques.
- **[Gerald Auger](https://www.youtube.com/c/GeraldAuger)**: Gerald provides insightful content on various cybersecurity topics, including certifications, career advice, and hands-on labs. His channel is a great resource for anyone looking to enhance their cybersecurity skills and knowledge.
- **[Symoné Beez](https://www.youtube.com/@symonebeez): Symoné shares information about govtech, if you're looking to get into the government tech space definitely check her out.
- **[Professor Messer](https://www.youtube.com/@professormesser): Professor Messer is the Internet's most comprehensive choice for CompTIA A+, Network+, Security+, and other IT certifications.


### Podcasts
- **[Darknet Diaries](https://darknetdiaries.com/)**: A podcast that tells true stories from the dark side of the internet.
- **[Security Now](https://www.grc.com/securitynow.htm)**: Covers the latest security news and topics, hosted by Steve Gibson.
- **[The CyberWire](https://www.cyberwire.com/podcasts/cyberwire-daily-briefing/)**: A daily podcast that discusses current cybersecurity news and trends.
- **[The InfoSec Podcast](https://www.infosecpodcast.com/)**: Focuses on various aspects of information security, featuring industry professionals and experts.
- **[Smashing Security](https://www.smashingsecurity.com/)**: A lighthearted take on cybersecurity news, with humor and expert insights.
